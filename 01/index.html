RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

Description: I thought the RO.ME demo was a very interesting and unique way to interact with music. I honestly haven't seen any other music video experiences similar to this one. The demo takes the user through 3 different settings:

  1. A broken down city where the users control animals roaming around.
  2. An open field where a black void of insects is controlled by the user.
  3. A vast desert where the user can freely control a flock of birds around floating masses of land.
Even for a demo made in 2011, this is still an impressive use of mixed media including 2D art, video, and 3D rendered graphics.
